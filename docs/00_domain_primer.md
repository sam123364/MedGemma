# T2D Domain Primer for Demo

## Core biomarkers
- HbA1c: long-horizon glucose exposure marker.
- Fasting glucose: short-horizon glycemic state.
- eGFR: kidney function proxy, important for medication safety.
- ALT: liver stress marker.

## Medication classes in this prototype
- Metformin: insulin sensitizer, baseline option if renal profile allows.
- GLP-1RA (e.g., semaglutide): glycemic and weight effects.
- SGLT2 inhibitors: cardio-renal supportive effects in many cohorts.
- Insulin/sulfonylurea: stronger glycemic drop but greater hypoglycemia concern.

## Why synthetic data
- Avoid patient privacy/compliance complexity.
- Keep challenge-safe and reproducible.

## What the app does not do
- No diagnosis.
- No treatment prescription.
- No real patient clinical support.
